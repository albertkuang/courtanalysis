## HOW TO ACCESS ROSTERS

Most D2 schools follow these URL patterns:
- **Standard Pattern 1**: `[schoolname]athletics.com/sports/mens-tennis/roster`
- **Standard Pattern 2**: `[schoolname].edu/athletics/sports/mens-tennis/roster`

**Examples:**
- Barry University: `gobarrybucs.com/sports/mens-tennis/roster`
- Wayne State: `wsuathletics.com/sports/mens-tennis/roster`
- Flagler College: `flaglerathletics.com/sports/mens-tennis/roster`
- Grand Valley State: `gvsulakers.com/sports/mens-tennis/roster`

**If the standard URL doesn't work:**
Search "[School Name] D2 men's tennis roster" to locate the specific page.
